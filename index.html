MAPPING OF KEYS
HOW IT WORKS


  • Use the keys on your keyboard to play our virtual piano. See the rules for mapping between your computer keys and the piano keys on our page before you start!


  • As you press keys on your keyboard, the piano keys light up and play the correspinding note for you! We have a standard 5-octave piano for you to create your music on!
